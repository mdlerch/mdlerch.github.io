Bridger Bowl Ski

Posted on 05 November 2017 in ski-log

After a nice early november snow in bozeman and the bridgers and a big gear stock up just a bit earlier, I was excited to get out for some early skiing. I was able to get out for a late afternoon ski at bridger bowl.

It was like a mid-season day up there with all the people and the snow. I hiked up to the top of pier's and skied down the trail the whole way. Not a very exciting ski, but was so nice to get out. First ski on the new dynafit bindings and dalbello boots.
